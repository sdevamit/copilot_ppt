What is GitHub Copilot?

<The_AI_Pair_Programmer />

Context-Aware Engine

Powered by multiple AI models (availability varies by plan and org configuration). Analyzes active files, cursor position, open tabs, and workspace context to infer intent—not just keywords.

Dynamic Learning

Synthesizes signals from your local project structure and coding patterns to generate idiomatic suggestions.

Multi-Modal Interaction

Ghost Text, Chat, Inline Edits, and Copilot Edits (multi-file agent) — choose the right mode for every task.

Strategic Goal

Reduces the "toil" of boilerplate so you can focus on design and complex logic.
