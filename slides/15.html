Prompt Principles

<Best_Practices_for_Developers />

Context is King

Copilot reads your open tabs. Keep relevant files open (interfaces, utils) to ground the AI in your codebase's reality.

Single Responsibility

One prompt, one goal. Ask for specific outcomes (e.g., "generate interface") rather than broad tasks to avoid hallucinations.

Explicit Constraints

Define the boundaries: "Use functional style", "No external libraries", "O(n) complexity", or "Follow Google Style Guide".

Acceptance Criteria

Tell Copilot how you will verify success: "Include unit tests for edge cases", "Generate DocBlock comments".

Iterative Refinement

Treat the first output as a draft. Review, then refine: "Now optimize for memory" or "Handle the null case".
